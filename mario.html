Mario


Occupation: Plumber/Womanizer

Quote: Wahoo!

Appearances: Super Mario series


Bio: Mario is a short and pudgy, Italian plumber who resides in the Mushroom Kingdom. His adventures consist upon rescuing Princess Peach from the Koopa villain Bowser. His younger brother, Luigi, likes to tag along but he usually is credited for Peach’s rescue. He enjoys karting and partying from time to time, even with Bowser.
